Our Story & Mission

Tapi Mai Shetkari Bachat Gat was established in 2010 with a vision to provide quality agricultural inputs at affordable prices to local farmers in the Tapi region. What began as a small cooperative of 50 farmers has now grown to serve over 2,000 farming families across the district.

We understand the challenges farmers face - rising input costs, unpredictable weather, and market fluctuations. Our mission is to help farmers overcome these challenges by providing:

Quality Assurance

Tested and certified fertilizers with no adulteration

Fair Pricing

Direct from manufacturers, eliminating middlemen

Timely Delivery

At your farm gate when you need it
